Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28/11/2025

CTrainer (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a mobile application designed to help users plan, track, and manage their workouts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using the CTrainer app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

CTrainer may collect the following information only if you explicitly provide it:

  • Exercise entries (sets, reps, weight)
  • Workout plans
  • Notes you add manually

We do not collect personal identity information such as your name, email, address, phone number, or photos.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Depending on your device and operating system settings, we may automatically collect:

  • Basic device information (model, operating system version)
  • App performance and usage data
  • Crash logs and error reports

This information is used solely to help diagnose issues and improve the app experience. It is not used to personally identify you.

1.3 No Sensitive Data Collected

CTrainer does not collect or request sensitive categories of data such as:

  • Health or medical records
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Payment or financial information
  • Biometric identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Fix bugs and enhance performance
  • Improve the overall user experience
  • Maintain the security and stability of the app

Workout data stored in the app remains on your device unless you choose to back it up, export it, or share it using your operating system’s features.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Workout and training data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not maintain our own remote database of your personal workout data.
  • If you uninstall the app, the data stored in the app on your device will be deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

CTrainer may use third-party services such as analytics or crash reporting tools to help us understand app performance and resolve technical issues.

These services may collect non-personal data such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash and error logs

We do not sell or rent your personal data and do not share personally identifiable information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Children’s Privacy

CTrainer is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the information stored about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data

Since CTrainer stores your workout data locally on your device, you can delete all stored data at any time by removing the app from your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of CTrainer after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
